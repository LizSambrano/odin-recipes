Sumi Salad

Description

This Sumi Salad is the best salad I have ever tried in my life! It is super easy to make yet super flavorful. I have enjoyed eating it in my family for years and I hope you enjoy it as well!

Recipe and Image Credit goes to: Lemon Tree Dwelling

Ingredients

  • 2 14 oz. packages coleslaw mix
  • 2 packages Ramen noodles, broken into small pieces (discard the seasoning packets)
  • 8 green onions, chopped
  • 1 cup sliced toasted almonds
  • 3/4 cup canola oil
  • 1/2 cup of rice wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. pepper

Steps

  1. Combine coleslaw mix and Ramen noodles in a large bowl.
  2. Add green onions and toasted almonds and mix.
  3. In a small pan, combine canola oil, rice wine vinegar, sugar, salt and pepper and bring to a boil. Stir until sugar dissolves. Remove it from heat.
  4. Let the dressing cool. Once cool, pour half over coleslaw mix and stir to combine.
  5. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
  6. Before serving, add the remaining dressing.
  7. Enjoy!

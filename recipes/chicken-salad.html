Chicken Salad

Description

This chicken salad is an authentic mexican style chicken salad. Growing up it was one of my mom's favorite dishes to make and whenever she took it to parties it was a big hit!

Recipe credit goes to: My Mom

Ingredients

  • 2 small bags of frozen mixed vegetables (corn, peas, carrots)
  • 2 large potatoes (peeled, and chopped into small cubes)
  • 1 container of mexican sour cream
  • 1 stalk of finely chopped celery
  • 1/2 of a cooked rotisserie style chicken (shredded, no skin)
  • salt and pepper (to taste)
  • corn tostadas
  • tapatio hot sauce (to taste)

Steps

  1. Steam the mixed vegetables according to the instructions.
  2. Boil the potatoes for 30-40 minutes.
  3. Put the steamed vegetables and the potatoes in a bowl and leave it in the fridge overnight.
  4. In the morning, mix the following into the vegetables/potatoes: sour cream, celery, chicken. Add salt and pepper according to taste.
  5. Once it is all mixed it it is ready to eat. Scoop a bit of the chicken salad onto a tostada and top it with some tapatio hot sauce. Yum!

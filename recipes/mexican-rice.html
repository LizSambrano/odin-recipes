Mexican Rice

Description

People may say that they know the secret to the best mexican rice. People may say that they finally found a restaurant with good quality mexican rice. However, this is by far the best rice you will ever try! It is a recipe handed down to me from my mom.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Mahatma: jasmine white rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 of an 8oz. can of tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp Knorr chicken stock
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 1/4 onion (diced)
  • vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Toast rice in small amount of oil until the rice is brown but not burnt.
  2. Once the rice is a caramel color, add in garlic and onion. Sizzle it a bit.
  3. Add in the water, Knorr chicken bouillon, salt, tomato sauce. Once this starts to boil, turn down the heat to low and cover. Let it cook for 20 minutes.
  4. Fluff it with a fork and serve.
